About

Virtual Choir Mixer was developed by Nick Cook, who is a member of the University of Michigan’s Men's Glee Club.  Nick, a computer science and linguistics major, built Virtual Choir Mixer so that choirs around the world can sing together while social distancing, without requiring complicated manual audio editing.


With the use of the Virtual Choir Mixer, the editing process is streamlined since there is no need to line up audio/video by ear—the computer does all the work.


Nick plans to implement more tools and techniques for making virtual choir videos more accessible, so that choirs that cannot rehearse together right now can collaborate and create beautiful music.


Feel free to contact the developer with feedback at nickcook@umich.edu
